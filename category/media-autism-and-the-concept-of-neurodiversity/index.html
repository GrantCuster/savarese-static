February 23rd, 2010

Listen to Emily and Ralph discuss the importance of the concept of neurodiversity on Iowa Public Radio’s “The Exchange.” Guests include two contributors to the special issue of DSQ: Jamie Burke, a student with autism at Syracuse University, and Scott Michael Robertson, a graduate student with Asperger syndrome at Penn State University and vice president of the Autistic Self-Advocacy Network (ASAN).
